[ COLOUR GUESSER ]

Summary:

This Project happened in a university module, and the given task was to programm a HEX-Code colour guessing game with Alpine.js.

There should be a random generated code, a choice of 3 different colours and a point system. Everytime you guessed a colour correctly you gained 1 point and every colour you guessed wrongly you lost 2 points. In addition you had a 10 second timer per choice

Test it here! I hope you enjoy!
